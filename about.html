About PeachtreeChat

PeachtreeChat is an Atlanta-based AI chatbot company focused on helping small businesses—like salons, real estate agents, and law firms—automate their customer interactions.

Founded with the belief that local businesses deserve the same cutting-edge tools as big tech, PeachtreeChat offers no-code, intelligent chatbots tailored to your needs.

Our mission is to save business owners time, improve client experience, and boost conversions—all through simple, smart automation.
